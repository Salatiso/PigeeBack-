What is PigeeBack?

PigeeBack is an innovative platform connecting people who need to send packages with those already travelling along the same routes. Whether you're an individual looking to make extra cash on your commute or a courier company aiming to fill empty space, PigeeBack is your solution.

Our vision is to transform the courier industry by making it more efficient, affordable, and sustainable. We put the power back in your hands, offering a win-win for both clients and service providers.

Gone are the days of expensive, rigid courier services and half-empty delivery vans. With PigeeBack, every journey has the potential to be a delivery opportunity!

How PigeeBack Works

For Clients (Sending a Package)

  1. Sign Up & Search: Register on PigeeBack and search for service providers travelling your desired route.
  2. Request Quotes: Connect with multiple providers to get the best price and service.
  3. Choose & Arrange: Select your preferred provider and arrange pickup/drop-off details directly.
  4. Track Your Package: Stay updated on your package's journey (feature dependent on provider).
  5. Save Money & Time: Enjoy cheaper, more flexible delivery options.

For Service Providers (Delivering Packages)

  1. Register & List Routes: Sign up and list your regular or planned travel routes.
  2. Receive Requests: Get notified by clients needing deliveries along your routes.
  3. Provide Quotes: Offer competitive pricing for your available space.
  4. Maximize Earnings: Turn empty space into profit and optimize your trips.
  5. Build Reputation: Gain positive reviews and attract more clients.

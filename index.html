What is PigeeBack?

PigeeBack is an innovative platform connecting people who need to send packages with those already travelling along the same routes. Whether you're an individual looking to make extra cash on your commute or a courier company aiming to fill empty space, PigeeBack is your solution.

Our vision is to transform the courier industry by making it more efficient, affordable, and sustainable. We put the power back in your hands, offering a win-win for both clients and service providers.

Gone are the days of expensive, rigid courier services and half-empty delivery vans. With PigeeBack, every journey has the potential to be a delivery opportunity!

How PigeeBack Works

For Clients (Sending a Package)

  1. Sign Up & Search: Register and find providers on your route.
  2. Request Quotes: Get the best price from multiple providers.
  3. Choose & Arrange: Select and coordinate directly.
  4. Track Your Package: Stay updated (provider dependent).
  5. Save Money & Time: Enjoy cheaper, flexible delivery.

For Service Providers (Delivering)

  1. Register & List Routes: Sign up and share your travel plans.
  2. Receive Requests: Get notified by clients on your routes.
  3. Provide Quotes: Offer competitive pricing.
  4. Maximize Earnings: Turn empty space into profit.
  5. Build Reputation: Gain reviews and attract more clients.
